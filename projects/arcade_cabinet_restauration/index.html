Arcade Cabinet Restoration

Restoration of an old arcade cabinet with some mebers of the Numérique pour tous assocation. The arcade cabinet is available for everyone at the library of Angers.

This arcade cabinet use a 19" LCD display and is running on a Raspberry Pi 3B (with Retropie), the buttons are directely plugged on the GPIO.
